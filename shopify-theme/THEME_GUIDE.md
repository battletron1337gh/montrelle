# Shopify Thema Customisatie

## Kleur Palet

### Primary
- **Zwart:** #0A0A0A (tekst)
- **Wit:** #FFFFFF (achtergrond)
- **Creme:** #F5F5F0 (accent achtergrond)

### Accent
- **Goud/Brons:** #C9A962 (CTA's, highlights)
- **Sage Groen:** #8FA895 (duurzaamheid accent)
- **Terracotta:** #C67B5C (limited edition, uitverkocht)

### Neutraal
- **Licht grijs:** #F7F7F7 (achtergronden)
- **Medium grijs:** #8A8A8A (subtekst)
- **Donker grijs:** #333333 (secundaire tekst)

## Typografie

### Headings
- **Font:** Playfair Display of Cormorant Garamond
- **Weight:** 500-600
- **Style:** Elegant, licht vintage

### Body
- **Font:** Inter of DM Sans
- **Weight:** 400-500
- **Size:** 16px base

### Accent
- **Font:** Zelfde als headings voor quotes/specials

## Componenten

### Buttons

**Primary (CTA)**
- Background: #0A0A0A
- Text: #FFFFFF
- Border-radius: 0 (sharp) of 2px (subtle)
- Padding: 16px 32px
- Hover: opacity 0.9, slight scale

**Secondary**
- Background: transparent
- Border: 1px solid #0A0A0A
- Text: #0A0A0A
- Hover: background #0A0A0A, text white

**Ghost (filters)**
- Background: #F7F7F7
- Text: #333333
- Active: background #0A0A0A, text white

### Cards

**Product Card**
- Image: aspect ratio 3:4
- Hover: quick shop button, image swap
- Badge: "Nieuw", "Bestseller", "-20%"
- Info: Title, subtitle, price (uitgelijnd)

### Forms

**Input Fields**
- Border: 1px solid #E0E0E0
- Border-radius: 0
- Focus: border #0A0A0A
- Padding: 12px 16px

## Animaties

**Micro-interactions**
- Hover op product: scale 1.02, duur 300ms, ease-out
- Button hover: translateY(-2px), shadow
- Add to cart: confetti of checkmark animatie
- Scroll: fade-in secties

**Page Transitions**
- Smooth scroll gedrag
- Fade tussen pagina's (indien mogelijk)

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
- Large: > 1440px

## Speciale Features

**Dark Mode Toggle** (optional)
- Background: #0A0A0A
- Text: #F5F5F0
- Accent: #C9A962

**Seasonal Themes**
- Spring: lichtere tinten, groen accent
- Summer: warm terracotta, beach vibes
- Autumn: diep bruin, oranje accent
- Winter: koel grijs, wit, goud
