## Technische Setup

### Shopify Apps Nodig:
1. **Oberlo/DSers** - Dropshipping automation
2. **Klaviyo** - Email marketing
3. **Judge.me** - Reviews
4. **Tidio** - Live chat
5. **OptinMonster** - Popups
6. **ReConvert** - Thank you page upsells
7. **Loox** - Photo reviews
8. **Frequently Bought Together** - Upsells

### Thema Keuze:
- **Dawn** (gratis) - Goede basis
- **Prestige** ($350) - Premium feel
- **Impulse** ($380) - Conversie geoptimaliseerd
- **Motion** ($350) - Modern & smooth
